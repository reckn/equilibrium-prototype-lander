Frequently asked questions

What does Equilibra do? +

Equilibra analyzes your customer and billing data to surface pricing and margin opportunities — underpriced accounts, negative-margin customers, excessive discounts, and renewal risk — then helps you act on them with experiments.

Who is Equilibra for? +

B2B SaaS finance, RevOps, and pricing teams who want to protect gross margin and grow ARR without guesswork.

How long does setup take? +

Connect your data sources and Equilibra builds your economic model automatically — most teams see their first opportunities the same day.

Is my data secure? +

Your data stays yours. Equilibra reads from your existing systems and never resells or shares your numbers. Enterprise controls are available for larger teams.
